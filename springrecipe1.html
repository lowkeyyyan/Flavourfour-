Parmesan Spring Chicken

A delicious, crispy dish perfect for the vibrant flavors of spring!

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh herbs for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Mix Parmesan, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Coat the chicken with olive oil, then dip in the Parmesan mixture.
  4. Place chicken pieces on a baking sheet and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden and crispy.
  5. Garnish with fresh herbs before serving. Enjoy!
