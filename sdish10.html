Mango Sorbet

A smooth pea soup, perfect for a spring day

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
White Fish Fillets (e.g., tilapia, cod) 4 fillets
Olive Oil 2 tbsp
Lemon Juice 2 tbsp
Garlic, minced 2 cloves
Fresh Parsley, chopped 1 tbsp
Fresh Dill, chopped 1 tbsp
Salt 1 tsp
Pepper 1/2 tsp
Optional: Lemon Slices 4 slices

Instructions

Step Action
1 Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
2 In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, fresh parsley, fresh dill, salt, and pepper.
3 Brush the fish fillets with the lemon herb mixture on both sides, making sure they are evenly coated.
4 Place the fish fillets on the preheated grill and cook for 3-4 minutes per side or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.
5 If desired, place lemon slices on the grill for 1-2 minutes to caramelize, and serve alongside the fish.
6 Serve immediately with your choice of side dishes. Enjoy!
