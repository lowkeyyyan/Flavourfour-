Parmesan Spring Chicken

A delicious, crispy dish perfect for the vibrant flavors of spring!

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Chicken Breasts 2 pieces
Parmesan Cheese 1 cup
Garlic 2 cloves
Spring Onions 1 bunch

Instructions

Step Action
1 Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2 Coat chicken in Parmesan cheese and spices.
3 Bake for 25 minutes until golden brown.
4 Garnish with spring onions and serve!
