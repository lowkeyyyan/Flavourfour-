Broccoli Cheese Soup

A Brocoli Cheese Soup filled with magical gold dust and healing factor

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Broccoli florets 4 cups
Butter 3 tbsp
Onion, diced 1 medium
Garlic, minced 2 cloves
All-purpose flour 1/4 cup
Chicken or vegetable broth 4 cups
Whole milk 2 cups
Shredded cheddar cheese 2 cups
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste

Instructions

Step Action
1 In a large pot, melt butter over medium heat. Add diced onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
2 Add minced garlic and cook for another minute, until fragrant.
3 Stir in flour and cook for 1-2 minutes to create a roux.
4 Slowly whisk in the broth and milk, ensuring no lumps form. Bring to a simmer.
5 Add broccoli florets and cook for 10-15 minutes, until broccoli is tender.
6 Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth, or leave it chunky if preferred.
7 Stir in shredded cheddar cheese until melted and smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
8 Serve hot and enjoy!
